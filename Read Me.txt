Case Report: Periocular Malignant Neoplasm

This repository contains supplementary material for a medical case report involving a 57-year old male presenting with lower eyelid basal cell carcinoma.

It includes anonymized data and supporting documentation such as the CARE Checklist.

Case Summary

- **Patient**: 57-year old male — anonymized 
- **Condition**: Lower eyelid basal cell carcinoma  
- **Diagnostic workup**: skin biopsy, histopathological examination using hematoxylin eosin staining
- **Outcome**: Surgical excision with good clinical outcomes
- **Key teaching point**: The lower eyelid is among high-risk sites for photoinduced skin cancer.
